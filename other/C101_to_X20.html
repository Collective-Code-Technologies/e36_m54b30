E30 C101: 22 GN/WS Reverse Light Switch (no EGS) Hot at run/start?  
  23 BR/GN Oil Pressure Switch Note 1:  
  24 WS/SW Econometer (MPG) 325ix ABS communication - the ABS computer monitors wheel speed sensors, engine load, RPM, TPS, etc, to detect excessive engine "compression braking".  If so, it tells Motronic 1.1/1.3 to regulate the ICV & retard timing, preventing wheel lockup due to lifting the throttle on low-traction surfaces (ice) or wheel spin due to excessive throttle.  GND with 1k+ resistor to preserve ABS function (10/1990 ix).  X20 #6 (ECU #64) and X20 #7 (ECU #66) are both uneeded connections from the X20 to the ECU, but can conflict with the diagnostics port.  I will connect it to a spare ADC port to datalog the output to the ECU.

  Note 4:
